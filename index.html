Floating Tower of Hanoi, 2017, Worcester Polytechnic Institute, MA link
I built this game with my friends in my first hackathon. During the hackathon, we were introduced to leap motion and found it fascinating, so we decided to build a game with it and choose the game floating tower of hanoi as the topic. Since my team all played this game in our childhood time, we designed it with lots of colors and pretty cute BGM. We mainly used three hand interactions: pick, point and release. While play's hand moves to one of the three sections, the current section will be highlighed and the other two will be blured.
